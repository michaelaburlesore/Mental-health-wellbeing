MENTAL HEALTH AND WELLBEING About us!

We have come together, to help people find a space, where they are able to access resources to enable them to get help with mental health issues, for self-care purposes. Whilst in a pandemic, it only seems appropriate to try and help tackle some of the problems people are facing. We are working alongside with Coding First Girls to try help this!
